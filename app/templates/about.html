The site

Welcome to Ringing Room! This is a site built for change ringers to continue ringing with one another even when socially distanced. It is under development currently, and new features may show up with little warning. Please contact us if you have any issues or suggestions.

You are using version {{version}}.

The developers

The Ringing Room is a joint venture by Leland Paul Reimer and Bryn Marie Reimer, both ringers in Northampton Massachusetts, USA.

The audio

The tower bell audio is provided by Bill Hibbert. The handbell recordings are of Leland's bells, with editing and extension by JES. Additional sound editing by Jack R Pease.
